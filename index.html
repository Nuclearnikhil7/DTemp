About us

Behind Every Temple keeps alive the timeless teachings and values of the Vedic tradition through donations and the sales of products and services that promote Sanatana Dharma,enhance Vedic education, and preserve our ancient temples.

The temple, which will come up on appx half an acre land

comprises of Garbhalayam, Antharalayam and Mahamandapam with large hall where about 300 devotees can wait to have the darshan of the Lord.

This is unprecedented and historical project attempted for the first time in the country with above mentioned temples in one campus.

Designs as mentioned in scriptures

The temple is constructed as per traditional Shilpa shastras

in the agama shastra style of architecture and is being built with an estimated cost of Rs. 150 Lakhs (1.5 crore)
